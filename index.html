Major Findings

  • Instagram and YouTube are the primary channels through which social media marketing reaches consumers in this demographic.
  • Influencer/creator posts are substantially more influential (39.5%) than traditional paid advertisements (9.3%).
  • Price Comparison: 62.8% agree that social media explicitly helps them compare prices and make better purchasing decisions.
  • Local Businesses: While 76.8% consider local Jhansi businesses to be somewhat or very effective on social media, there's a conversion gap needing better content strategy (only 39.5% purchase rate).
